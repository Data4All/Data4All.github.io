The Team

We are a small team consisting of Bachelor students who are working on providing an easy to use tool for geographical information mapping.

Our Inspiration

The availabilty of geographical data is crucial for political and economical development. Unfortunately there are a lot of places in the world where this information is not yet available. So far collecting, editing and providing this information needs fundamental technical knowledge. We want to help change the current situation in making this process a lot easier.

Our Product

Our application aims to provide an easy to use solution to collect and map geographical information. It offers the possibility to download maps from OSM prior to mapping the desired area to avoid connection issues during the process. You can also collect information in groups using a peer 2 peer connection. Our application allows you to take pictures of the desired area, record voice notes to accompany collected data and to edit everything on the go.

Our aim is to map the world.

What we aim to achieve

We want to provide an application that allows EVERYONE to collect geographical information.

Record voice notes.

Record voice notes while you collect data to ensure accuracy.

Snap pictures.

You can take photos and trace everything you want to map directly on them.

Edit on the go.

Our application allows you to edit maps provided by OpenStreetMap wherever you are.

Connect with others.

Don't want to go alone? You can always take people with you and map together via a peer 2 peer connection.
